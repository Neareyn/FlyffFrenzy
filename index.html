Welcome to Flyff Frenzy - Trivia Takeoff! (v0.1.2-alpha)

Flyff Frenzy is a small quiz game that tests players' knowledge of Flyff skills.
Players are presented with an image of a skill and four different names, and they have to click on the correct name corresponding to the skill.

This is an alpha version. See skills that shouldn't be in here? Message Nero#7295 on Discord.
